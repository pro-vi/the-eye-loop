## Stage Rules → REWRITE

**Before:** swipe-count thresholds (1-4 words, 5-8 images, 9-14 mockups).

**After:** Scout chooses format based on evidence depth. Oracle sets a concreteness FLOOR as guardrail:
- evidence.length < 5 → floor = words
- 5-8 → floor = images (scout may choose mockup if it feels confident)
- 9-14 → floor = mockups
- >= 15 → trigger reveal

The floor prevents regression (can't go back to words at swipe 10) but doesn't force escalation (scout at swipe 6 can still choose a word if that's most informative).

---

## Anima Model → REPLACE

**Before:** flat list of 5-7 axes seeded from intent, YAML serialized with distributions.

**After:** growing evidence list. No seeding — first scout probe comes from intent alone. Anti-patterns extracted by builder from rejections.

The Anima is not stored as a data structure. It's the LLM's inference from the evidence corpus, recomputed every time a scout or builder reads the context.

---

## Scout Loop → REWRITE step 1-2

**Before:**
1. Read weakest axis (lowest confidence)
2. Generate facade targeting that axis

**After:**
1. Read evidence history + any builder probe brief
2. Generate facade targeting the biggest gap in taste knowledge (LLM decides, not code)

Steps 3-5 (push to queue, wait for swipe, update status) unchanged.
